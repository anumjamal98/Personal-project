Our Story

Crafting unforgettable fragrances since 2010

The Essentae Journey

Founded in 2010, Essentae began as a small boutique perfumery with a big dream: to create fragrances that tell stories.

Our founder, Mr. Amir Akram, a master perfumer with over 20 years of experience, believed that a fragrance should be more than just a scent—it should be an experience, a memory, an emotion.

Starting with just three signature scents, we've grown into a beloved brand with over 50 unique fragrances, each carefully crafted using the finest natural ingredients sourced from around the world.

Today, Essentae is not just a perfume brand—it's a celebration of individuality, luxury, and the art of perfumery.
